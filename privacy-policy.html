BlockU — Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-04-30 · Publisher: Shinguru Games · Contact: omer19109@gmail.com

BlockU ("the Game") is a logic puzzle game published by Shinguru Games ("we", "us"). This Privacy Policy describes what data the Game collects, how it is used, and your rights. We have designed BlockU to collect as little data as possible.

What we do not collect

  • We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, or any other personally identifiable information (PII).
  • We do not require an account to play.
  • We do not track your location.
  • We do not access your contacts, photos, microphone, or camera.

What we do collect

1. Anonymous gameplay analytics (GameAnalytics)

The Game uses GameAnalytics to understand how players engage with the game so we can improve it. This includes:

  • Device type, operating system, app version, language
  • Game events (level started, level completed, hints used, errors)
  • Anonymous session identifiers

This data is anonymous — it cannot be linked to you personally. GameAnalytics's own privacy policy governs their handling: https://gameanalytics.com/privacy

2. Advertising (Google AdMob)

The Game shows ads via Google AdMob. AdMob may collect:

  • Your device's Advertising ID (a resettable identifier you can manage in your device settings)
  • Network and device information
  • Ad interaction data (impressions, clicks)

This data is used by Google to serve relevant ads and measure ad performance. In the European Economic Area, United Kingdom, and Switzerland, the Game shows a consent dialog on first launch where you can choose between personalized and non-personalized ads. You can change your choice later in the device's privacy settings.

Google's policies govern this data: https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads

3. In-app purchases (Google Play Billing)

If you make a purchase, the transaction is handled entirely by Google Play. We receive only the entitlement (e.g., "Remove Ads is owned"); we do not receive your payment details, name, or address. Google's purchase data is governed by their policies.

4. Local storage on your device

The Game stores the following on your device only — never transmitted to our servers:

  • Your level progress, stars earned, and best times
  • Your game settings (sound, music, language, theme, accessibility options)
  • Purchase entitlement flags (e.g., "ads removed")
  • Anonymous session counters

You can delete all this data at any time by clearing the app's data in your device settings or uninstalling the Game.

5. Anonymous level feedback (optional)

If you rate a procedurally generated Endless mode level, the Game sends only the level seed and your rating ("liked" or "disliked") to our server. No identifying information is sent. This helps us rank and surface better levels.

Children's privacy

BlockU is rated for all ages but is not specifically directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided data through this Game, please contact us at the address below and we will remove it.

Data retention

We retain anonymous analytics data for the period required to operate and improve the Game. Because we do not collect PII, we cannot identify you in that data. To erase all locally stored data, uninstall the Game or clear its app data in your device settings.

Your rights (GDPR / CCPA)

If you are in the European Economic Area, United Kingdom, Switzerland, or California, you have the right to:

  • Request information about the data collected about you
  • Request correction or deletion of that data
  • Withdraw consent for personalized advertising at any time (via the in-app consent dialog or your device's privacy settings)
  • Lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority

Because BlockU does not collect personally identifiable information, we generally cannot link any data we have to you specifically. To exercise these rights or ask questions, email us at the address below.

Third-party services used by BlockU

  • Google AdMob — advertising
  • GameAnalytics — anonymous analytics
  • Google Play Billing — in-app purchases (Android only)
  • Our level pool service — anonymous endless mode level rankings (no PII)

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as the Game evolves. The updated policy will be posted at this URL with a revised "Effective date". Material changes will be communicated in the Game release notes.
